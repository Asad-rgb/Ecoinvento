Effortless Detection: A Quick Overview

  • Visit the Website

    Click the "Visit" button in the header to open the plant disease detection dashboard. This user-friendly page gives you access to all tools and features, designed to work smoothly.

  • Upload a Leaf Image

    Choose or capture a clear photo of the affected leaf from your device. High-quality images ensure better and more accurate detection of disease symptoms like- insect, funges etc.

    Your results appear within seconds, showing labeled problem areas with accuracy percentages—no expert knowledge required. it also suggests eco-friendly, bio-based treatment.

Faq questions

Questions? Look here.

What does this website do?

This website allows users to upload images of plant leaves to detect diseases and insect damage using AI-powered image analysis. It identifies over 14,000 species with high accuracy and suggests natural treatments.

How accurate is the detection system?

Our system is powered by the Kindwise API and delivers up to 92% accuracy based on validated agricultural datasets and real-time model learning.

Do I need to create an account to use it?

No account is required. Simply visit the website, upload your image, and get instant results without any sign-up process.

What kind of images should I upload?

Upload clear, focused images of plant leaves showing visible symptoms like yellow spots, holes, or fungus. Higher quality images result in better detection accuracy.

Does the website suggest treatment options?

Yes. After detection, the website offers bio-based treatment suggestions like neem oil, compost tea, or natural pest repellents to support sustainable farming.

Is the service free to use?

Yes, the service is currently free for educational and research purposes, especially for students, farmers, and garden enthusiasts.

Who created this website and which technologies were used?

This website was created by "Asad Madni" a Class 9 student as part of a science project to promote smart farming and AI-based agriculture. It uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the frontend and Python (Flask) for the backend.

This website is a smart and easy-to-use tool for detecting plant diseases and insect damage just by uploading a photo of a leaf. Powered by the Kindwise API, it can recognize over 14,000+ species.
